About PassX

PassX is a personal password manager designed to help users securely store, manage, and access their passwords across various websites and platforms.

Our Mission

At PassX, we aim to simplify digital security by offering an easy-to-use tool that helps you store and manage your passwords efficiently.

Why Use PassX?

  • Simple, user-friendly interface
  • Secure password storage using local storage
  • Quick access to your saved credentials
  • Clipboard copy for easy access

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. PassX stores all passwords locally on your device, ensuring that only you have access to them.
